Muhammad Ali Jinnah

The man who freed billion lives.

Here's a time line of Quaid e Azam's life:

  • 1876 - Born in Karachi, Bombay, British India
  • 1895 - Jinnah traveled to London to study law. University of Minnesota, thanks to a Depression era program known as the "National Youth Administration"
  • 1913 - Jinnah became the leader of the All-India Muslim League.
  • 1940 - Jinnah proposed the two-nation theory, which called for the creation of a separate Muslim state.
  • 1947 - Pakistan was created on August 14, 1947, and Jinnah became its first governor-general.
  • 1948 - Jinnah passed away on September 11, 1948, in Karachi, Pakistan.
